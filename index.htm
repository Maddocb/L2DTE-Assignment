Purpose

The purpose of the Waste punawananga is to introduce robotics and digital technology through the use of making repurposed, old or unused technology into new creations.
Our Puna group has been working to create various projects over this year that have been using recycled laptop screens, led lights, and vex v5 robotic equipment.
